Lasagna

Description

A classic recipe of one of the most loved foods in the world. The ultimate comfort food = homemade lasagna. Family and friends gather around the dinner table at any time for this lasagna recipe.

A big warm comforting hug within layers of pasta, tomato sauce, white sauce and melted cheese!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1-1/4 pounds bulk Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2/3 cup water
